Sony Services Centre opened in 1995. Sony Services Centre prides itself in being a "do all" service center by being able to adapt and change to the situation on hand. Being a family business,our vast experience in the industry along with our relationships with dealers, manufacturers and service contract companies enable us to excel on a regional level through our in-home service and dealer/depot repair departments.
